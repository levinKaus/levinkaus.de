About Me

Hi, my name is Levin, and I am currently a first-year master's student in Computer Science and Business Economics at Technische Universität Darmstadt.

During high school, I discovered my passion for computer science after taking my first IT class, where I was captivated by the limitless potential of this field. This interest was further piqued during a six-month internship at a startup, where I was introduced to the world of business economics, ultimately inspiring me to pursue combined studies including both disciplines at university.

Currently, I have a strong interest in iOS development. After learning Swift and applying my skills during my internship at INVENSITY, I am now working on my own iOS app. Stay tuned for more updates in the "Works" section.

Outside of academics, I enjoy traveling, photography, riding my bike and going to the cinema. Looking ahead, I aim to become an entrepreneur and make a meaningful contribution to the tech industry. I’m eager to continue my studies and see where my career takes me.
